1. Logo

no.socks

Primary Wordmark

Minimalist, confident, and clever. The wordmark is lowercase to feel approachable and modern. The period (.) acts as a visual anchor and a nod to our digital-first, direct approach. It feels more like a signature than a corporate stamp.
